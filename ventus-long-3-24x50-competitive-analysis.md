# Ventus Long-Range 3-24× / 4-24× × 50mm Class — Competitive Analysis

**Class:** Long-range FFP/SFP, 3-24× / 4-24× magnification, 50mm objective, **30mm tube**
**Ventus SKUs in class:** 4 (FFP 3-24×50, FFP 4-24×50, SFP 3-24×50, SFP 4-24×50)

> **Tube-strict comparison.** Ventus is 30mm in this class. Most long-range FFP scopes at 24× migrate to 34mm for the elevation travel. 30mm-tube peers are few. Non-30mm competitors (Vector Continental 4-24×56 at 34mm, Vortex Razor Gen II 4.5-27×56 at 34mm, Arken SH-4 at 34mm, Athlon Cronus at 34mm, S&B PM II 3-20×50 Ultra Short at 34mm, Burris XTR III at 34mm) are excluded from direct comparison.

---

## 1. TL;DR — Main Findings

1. **Ventus 32 MIL elevation on a 30mm tube is class-leading among 30mm-tube 50mm-objective FFP** at 24× magnification. Nightforce NX8 4-32×50 F1 (the only other 30mm-tube tactical in class) offers 26 MIL; Vortex Viper PST Gen II 5-25×50 offers 20 MIL.
2. **Ventus 26.2 MIL windage beats both 30mm peers** (NF NX8 20 MRAD; Vortex PST 10 MRAD).
3. **Ventus 3-24× zoom with 3× low-end** is structurally unique — Nightforce starts at 4× (or 2.5× on the 2.5-20 model); Vortex PST starts at 5×.
4. **Weight 792g (3-24×50)** is the lightest 30mm-tube FFP with 30+ MIL elevation in class.
5. **Turret spec** — 0.1 MIL / 1/4 MOA dual, locking + zero stop, side focus 15yds–∞.
6. **Realistic retail peer** — Nightforce NX8 4-32×50 F1 (~$2,450) on elevation; Vortex Viper PST Gen II 5-25×50 FFP (~$999–1,099) on zoom pattern.

## 6. Competitive Position Summary

### Where Ventus wins (on paper, tube-strict)

- **Elevation** — 32 MIL leads 30mm-tube 50mm FFP class.
- **Windage** — 26.2 MIL leads class.
- **Weight (3-24× SKU)** — lightest with 30+ MIL elevation in class.
- **3× low-end zoom** — uncontested in 30mm FFP at this objective.
- **Dual click option** — 0.1 MIL or 1/4 MOA.
- **Side focus 15 yds** — closer than most 30mm long-range competitors.
- **Price class** — sub-$1000 OEM vs $2,450 (NF NX8) or $999–1,099 (Vortex PST).

### Where Ventus has risk / uncertainty

- **32 MIL elevation on 30mm** — at the physical ceiling. Independent tracking test recommended.
- **Optical quality at 24×** — no public reviews of Jupiter 3-24 or Venus 4-24 glass.
- **30mm tube in long-range class** — many PRS buyers expect 34mm; Ventus's tube spec may face bias even with superior travel figures.
- **No published MTBF or warranty data.**

### Realistic retail peers

- **Nightforce NX8 4-32×50 F1 ($2,450):** tube and objective-class peer. Ventus wins elevation (32 vs 26 MIL), windage (26.2 vs 20 MIL), and price (~3× cheaper); NF wins proven tracking, glass pedigree, and 32× top magnification.
- **Vortex Viper PST Gen II 5-25×50 FFP ($999–1,099):** price peer. Ventus wins decisively on elevation (32 vs 20 MIL), windage (26.2 vs 10 MIL), and low-end zoom (3× vs 5×). Vortex wins on US warranty + reticle range.

For 34mm-tube alternatives (S&B PM II 3-20, Athlon Cronus, Arken SH-4, Burris XTR III, Vortex Razor) — see the 34mm tube-class analyses. Those are separate engineering classes.
