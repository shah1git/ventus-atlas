# Ventus LPVO 1-10× Class — Competitive Analysis

**Class:** Low-Power Variable Optic, 1-10× magnification, 24/26/28mm objective, 30mm or 34mm tube
**Ventus SKUs in class:** 4 (FFP 1-10×26, FFP 1-10×24, SFP 1-10×28, SFP 1-10×24) — "Jupiter" series

> **Tube-strict comparison.** Ventus offers both 30mm and 34mm tubes in this class, so the analysis is split into sub-classes. Competitors are compared only against Ventus SKUs sharing the same tube diameter.

---

## 1. TL;DR — Main Findings

1. **30mm sub-class:** Ventus 34.9 MIL elevation sits behind Primary Arms GLx (40 MIL) as the only other current 30mm FFP 1-10×. PA GLx wins on elevation and click value; Ventus wins on weight.
2. **34mm sub-class:** Ventus 34.9 MIL elevation beats every current 34mm 1-10× FFP — Vortex Razor Gen III (30 MIL), Athlon Ares ETR Gen2 (29 MIL), Trijicon Credo HX (29 MIL). Vector Continental x10 (51 MIL) leads but on a different zoom-ratio design.
3. **Ventus 30mm SKUs at 527g / 553g** are the lightest FFP 1-10× offerings on either tube — PA SLx (542g SFP) and Trijicon Credo HX (601g) are the nearest peers.
4. **1/2 MOA click** is the persistent class-wide weakness.
5. **Published FOV @ 1×** exceeds every competitor on both tubes — flagged for independent verification.
6. **Realistic retail peer:** 30mm — Primary Arms GLx 1-10×24 FFP (~$680). 34mm — Trijicon Credo HX 1-10×28 FFP (~$1,429–1,600).

## 6. Competitive Position Summary

### Where Ventus wins (on paper, tube-strict)

- **34mm sub-class:** Ventus FFP 1-10×26 elevation (34.9 MIL) beats Vortex Razor Gen III, Athlon, and Trijicon Credo HX — the entire current Western premium 34mm 1-10× field outside Vector Continental.
- **34mm sub-class:** Weight (608g) ties with Trijicon Credo HX (601g) as one of the lightest FFP 1-10× on 34mm.
- **30mm sub-class:** Weight (527g / 553g) is lightest FFP 1-10× anywhere.
- **Both sub-classes:** FOV @ 1× claim is largest published.
- **Price tier:** Sub-$500 OEM vs $680 (PA GLx) or $1,000–$2,000 (34mm premium).

### Where Ventus loses / carries risk

- **30mm sub-class:** PA GLx beats Ventus on elevation (40 vs 34.9 MIL) and click precision.
- **34mm sub-class:** Vector Continental x10 dominates on elevation (51 vs 34.9 MIL).
- **Both:** 1/2 MOA click is the class-wide precision weakness.
- **SFP 1-10×28:** 26.2 MIL elevation is lowest in 34mm field. Separate marketing message required.
- **FOV claim** exceeds even Chinese-OEM peers — needs verification.
- **No public reviews, MTBF, warranty data.**

### Realistic retail peers

- **30mm:** Primary Arms GLx 1-10×24 FFP ($680). Ventus must undercut on price to win.
- **34mm:** Trijicon Credo HX 1-10×28 FFP ($1,429–1,600). Ventus wins on elevation and click-price ratio; Credo wins on glass and warranty.
