# Ventus Mid-Range 2-12× / 2-16× × 44mm Class — Competitive Analysis

**Class:** Mid-range FFP/SFP, 2-12× / 2-16× magnification, 44mm objective, **30mm tube**
**Ventus SKUs in class:** 4 (FFP 2-16×44, FFP 2-12×44, SFP 2-16×44, SFP 2-12×44)

> **Tube-strict comparison.** Ventus is 30mm in this class. Only 30mm-tube competitors are included below. 34mm and 35mm scopes (Vector Continental x6 2-12×44 FFP, Athlon Ares ETR 3-18×50, Arken SH-4, Leupold Mark 5HD, Bushnell Match Pro ED, Burris XTR III) are structurally different engineering classes and have been excluded from direct comparison.

---

## 1. TL;DR — Main Findings

1. **Ventus 49.5 MIL elevation is class-leading by a wide margin on 30mm tube.** The next-highest 30mm-tube 44mm-obj FFP in class is Vector Continental x8 SFP at 32 MIL. Vortex Viper PST Gen II 3-15×44 FFP offers only 22 MIL. Athlon Midas TAC offers ~24 MIL.
2. **Ventus 40.7 MIL windage is class-leading by ~27% over the next-best** (Vector x8 SFP at 32 MIL). Vortex (11 MIL) and Athlon Midas TAC (16 MIL) are far below.
3. **2-16× zoom range is uncontested.** No other 30mm-tube FFP on the market offers a 2× low-end at 44mm objective — Vortex PST starts at 3×, Athlon Midas TAC at 4×.
4. **Weight 710–715g is competitive** — lighter than Vortex PST (797g), similar to Athlon Midas TAC (675g).
5. **Turret package is complete:** 0.1 MIL / 1/4 MOA dual click, locking + zero stop, side focus 10yds–∞.
6. **Realistic retail peer** — Vortex Viper PST Gen II 3-15×44 FFP (~$899–999) as direct 30mm FFP competitor; Athlon Midas TAC Gen2 HD 4-16×44 FFP (~$600–700) at lower price point.

## 6. Competitive Position Summary

### Where Ventus wins (on paper)

- **Elevation** — 49.5 MIL is highest by a wide margin in 30mm class.
- **Windage** — 40.7 MIL is 3.7× Vortex PST.
- **Zoom range** — 2× low-end available only from Vector (and Ventus) at 30mm.
- **FFP availability** — Vector equivalent is SFP only.
- **Click flexibility** — 0.1 MIL or 1/4 MOA dual option.
- **Price class** — sub-$900 OEM vs $899–999 Vortex.

### Where Ventus has risk / uncertainty

- **49.5 MIL elevation on a 30mm tube** is at the physical ceiling — worth independent tracking verification.
- **Weight** — not best-in-class; Vector/Athlon are ~40g lighter.
- **Optical quality / tracking / warranty** — no public reviews.

### Realistic retail peers

- **Vortex Viper PST Gen II 3-15×44 FFP ($899–999):** direct FFP peer on 30mm. Ventus wins elevation/windage/zoom-range; Vortex wins proven tracking + US warranty.
- **Athlon Midas TAC Gen2 HD 4-16×44 FFP ($600–700):** direct price peer. Ventus wins elevation/windage/zoom; Midas TAC wins weight + market track record.
- **Vector Continental x8 2-16×44 SFP ED ($449–629):** same zoom range but SFP. Ventus wins FFP availability and elevation; Vector wins price and weight.
