# Ventus Long-Range 3.125-25× / 5-25× × 50mm Class — Competitive Analysis

**Class:** Long-range FFP/SFP, 3.125-25× / 5-25× magnification, 50mm objective, **30mm tube**
**Ventus SKUs in class:** 4 (FFP 3.125-25×50, FFP 5-25×50, SFP 3.125-25×50, SFP 5-25×50)

> **Tube-strict comparison.** Ventus is 30mm in this class. Non-30mm competitors (Athlon Cronus BTR 34mm, Arken SH-4 34mm, S&B PM II 5-25 34mm, Burris XTR III 34mm) are excluded. Only 30mm-tube peers appear below.

---

## 1. TL;DR — Main Findings

1. **Ventus 32 MIL elevation ties Nightforce NX8 2.5-20×50 F1 exactly** — the two scopes define the 30mm-tube elevation ceiling in this magnification class. NF NX8 costs ~3× any expected Ventus retail.
2. **Ventus 26.2 MIL windage is class-leading on 30mm** — beats NF NX8 (23 MRAD), Athlon Midas TAC (25 MIL), Vortex Viper PST (10 MRAD), Vector Continental SFP (16.5 MIL).
3. **Weight 765g is class-leading** for 30+ MIL elevation on 30mm. Only Athlon Midas TAC (745g, lower elevation) and Vector Continental SFP (745g, much lower elevation) are lighter.
4. **3.125× low-end zoom on 8× zoom ratio** is unique in 30mm-tube 50mm-obj FFP at this magnification.
5. **Turret package complete:** 0.1 MIL / 1/4 MOA, locking + zero stop, side focus 15yds–∞.
6. **Realistic retail peers:** Athlon Midas TAC 6-24×50 FFP (~$690) and Vortex Viper PST Gen II 5-25×50 FFP (~$999–1,099) at direct price tier; Nightforce NX8 at 2–3× price.

## 6. Competitive Position Summary

### Where Ventus wins (on paper, tube-strict)

- **Elevation** — ties NF NX8 at 32 MIL, class-ceiling.
- **Windage** — 26.2 MIL is class-leading.
- **Weight** — 765g is very competitive; among lightest FFP with 32 MIL in class.
- **3.125× low-end zoom** — unique in class.
- **Dual click option** — 0.1 MIL or 1/4 MOA.
- **Price class** — sub-$1000 OEM vs $2,250+ NF NX8 / $999+ Vortex PST.

### Where Ventus has risk / uncertainty

- **32 MIL on 30mm** — at physical ceiling; tracking verification recommended.
- **Against NF NX8** — NF has 2.5× low-end and 20× top-end (shorter zoom range). Ventus covers wider range with 3.125-25×; for 24–25× use, it's a direct alternative.
- **Optical quality / tracking / warranty** — no public reviews.

### Realistic retail peers

- **Nightforce NX8 2.5-20×50 F1 ($2,250–2,600):** elevation-tied peer. Ventus wins windage (+3.2 MIL), weight (−37g), zoom range (3.125-25 vs 2.5-20), and price (~3× cheaper). NF wins glass pedigree, tracking proven, US service.
- **Athlon Midas TAC 6-24×50 FFP ($687–700):** direct price peer. Ventus wins elevation (32 vs 25 MIL), windage (26.2 vs 25 MIL), low-end zoom (3.125 vs 6). Midas TAC wins on proven market track record.
- **Vortex Viper PST Gen II 5-25×50 FFP ($999–1,099):** price peer. Ventus wins everything on spec — elevation, windage, weight, zoom range.

For 34mm-tube alternatives (S&B PM II 5-25, Athlon Cronus, Arken SH-4, Burris XTR III) — see 34mm-specific analyses.
